Protégé-OWL ontology editor and knowledge-base framework.

Email bugs, questions, and comments to protege-owl.

Protégé-OWL is developed at the Stanford Center for Biomedical Informatics Research. Some toolbar icons were inspired by those from Eclipse and IntelliJ IDEA. For more information, see the Protégé website.

Open source from: http://smi-protege.stanford.edu/repos/protege/owl/trunk/
